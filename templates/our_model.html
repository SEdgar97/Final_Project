ABOUT THE MODEL

Using the most common standard practice within the machine learning world, our model takes into account the binary symptom and classed diagnosis data in order to output the user’s prognosis. With an accuracy score of 100% in the training and test data, our model has a credible and reliable system of diagnosing a user’s symptoms. By integrating a more in depth predict method within the model, it not only determines the most likely diagnosis, but the probability of the top three diagnoses as well.
