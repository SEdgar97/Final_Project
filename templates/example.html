Our Model

If we have taken anything out of the past year, it's a compulsive urge to keep ourselves healthy and monitor any sign of disease. We fear even the smallest of symptoms because they could be the first sign of something serious. This fear is in competition with our increasingly strong desire to remain inside and isolated from the general public in places like doctor's offices. If only there was a way to understand what our symptoms mean from the comfort of our homes?

This model was trained using a dataset of ~4000 people with 41 different diagnosable conditions. Simply click the 'Enter Symptoms' button below and you will be prompted to choose from 132 different potential symptoms that you may be experiencing. The model will diagnose the most likely disease to match the symptoms and will also provide a set of the three most likely diagnoses so that you can explore different opinions.

The Our Model tab above will explain more how the model works and the work that went into developing it. The Disease Index tab will take you to a full repository outlining further details and useful links to learn more information about each of the 41 different conditions in our dataset.
